Network Monitor.

Network monitor is your best application ever to know if you can access the Internet or not. It doesn't care if you're on WiFi, protected WiFi (such as hotels, planes and coffee shops) or corporate network. All that matters is that your computer can reach the Internet and that you know whether or not it's working!

If you are facing any issues, please refer to the Support page or place an issue on github. That being said, you can also always post on Twitter with to the @NetworkMonitorMac account.

Finally, if you are concerned about privacy regarding this application, the Privacy page has more detailed information about what Network Monitor discloses from your computer. The short version is: your IP address (and nothing else) to a single server (4.2.2.2).
